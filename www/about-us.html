About Us

Quiddi Compare was set up in 2012 and is currently one of the largest loan and finance comparison sites in the UK. Over the years, we have grown and introduced new loan products including payday loans and logbook loans and different money products such as mortgages and bank accounts.

What we do

We’ve read a lot in the press about loan brokers and how they charge hidden fees and pass on your details to third parties. Quiddi Compare was set up to offer a completely transparent solution to compare the best financial products in the UK and allow you to work directly with the companies. Above all, recent studies have shown that using comparison sites can save an individual hundreds of pounds a year on basic financial products.

As a leading comparison site, we have carefully selected the partners we work with to provide the cheapest and most responsible financial products. Our dedicated team is constantly updating our site with new partners to ensure our users are getting the best deals available on the market.

What we offer

We feature the best lenders on our comparison tables and allow the borrower to compare the cost of the loan or financial product by comparing the APR and representative example. Customers can also use our special ‘Smart Search’ tool where they can fill in a few basic details to find the best product specifically for them and the percentage that they are likely to be approved.

Borrowers can then apply directly on the safe and secure site of one of our partners. Quiddi Compare will never charge the customer any fees for using our service. Instead, we prefer to take a commission from the lender or provider as an introductory fee and this makes it completely free for customers to use.

We are able to provide financial options to customers who have bad credit and have been turned down by other traditional banks and lenders. Specifically, our guarantor loans and credit cards for bad credit give customers an opportunity to get the finance they need and also build their credit rating.

As part of our commitment to responsible lending, we offer regular money saving and personal finance tips on our blog and social media. You can keep up to date by following us on Facebook and Twitter and be sure to get involved in our regular competitions and giveaways too!.
