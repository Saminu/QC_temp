What They offer
They simplify your loan search and application process to provide an efficient and accurate experience. They will provide you with free accurate loan quotes directly from their partnered lenders, which are tailor made to meet your individual circumstances. Your credit rating will not be affected until you make a firm application for a loan; the free quotation search has no impact at all.

  • Efficient and accurate service

  • Actual interest rates based on individual circumstances

  • Free accurate loan quote with no credit footprint

How it works
Lenders have rules by which they determine rates to assign to a specific borrower. These rules have been shared with them, which means that they can give you free accurate loan quotes on behalf of the lender without affecting your credit rating. If you want to apply, they send the details to your lender on your behalf so you don't need to re-enter any information. The lender will then contact you to confirm that the loan has been accepted, and arrange the transfer of money.

  • Accurate quote

  • No credit footprint

  • No need to retype information

  • Lender contacts borrower to arrange the transfer

What it costs
There income is generated from the lenders who they charge for completing the loan. There is a processing fee attached to some loans but this is included in all the quotes that they present to you. The fee covers the
cost of property valuation, the reference from your mortgage company, any additional legal and administrative costs. This is not payable upfront but instead is added to your loan on its completion.

Footprint free!
They use leading credit reference agency Equifax to provide them with 'soft' credit searches that do not impact your credit rating. The 'soft' footprints left by their free quotation search can only be seen by you.
No one else can view this activity on your credit report. If you decide to make a formal application for credit, then your lender may impose a 'hard' footprint on your credit file in response to this.
