Gissaloan Logbook Loans

London-based Gissaloan offer logbook loans in the UK which allow you to borrow money for up to 24 months. Customers are required to secure the loan with their car so if you are unable to meet repayments, your car may be repossessed by the lender to cover the cost of the loan. But by putting your car down as collateral, customers with bad credit are able to gain access to finance that would otherwise be inaccessible with mainstream lenders.

Customers can apply via mobile, desktop or in person with Gissaloan provided they make an appointment. Applicants will be required to send in paperwork regarding their vehicle and their income prior to approval but provided this can be sent quickly, Gissaloan offer same day approval and funding for their logbook loans.

How Much Can You Borrow With Gissaloan Logbook Loans?

Customers applying for logbook loans with Gissaloan can borrow between £500 and £25,000. How much a customer can borrow will depend on the value of their vehicle which they are putting down as collateral. Typically, the newer the car, the more it is worth and this will increase your borrowing facility.

Other factors that contribute towards how much you can borrow include your level of affordability and credit rating. Those customers with higher levels of income and a good credit history will be eligible to borrow more than others.

What Is The Criteria For a Gissaloan Logbook Loan?

To be eligible for a Gissaloan logbook loan, customers must be willing to put down their car as collateral. The car must be less than 12 years old and free from finance or nearly free. Customers must be in employment and self-employed applicants are eligible too.

Applicants will be required to provide the following:

  • V5 logbook in their name for a minimum of 3 months
  • Up to date MOT
  • Tax certificate
  • Full UK driving license
  • 3 years address history
  • Pay-slip or bank statement
  • 2 up to date proofs of address

A ‘bill of sale’ must be signed by the customer and this legally transfers the ownership of the vehicle to Gissaloan who will hold on it until the loan has been repaid. Customers are still able to drive the car as usual during the loan term.

How Does Repayment Work For Gissaloan Logbook Loans?

Repayments are collected automatically every week or month, depending on your loan repayment terms.

Here is a repayment example: Loan Amount: £1000.00. Loan Term, 78 weeks. Instalments: £30.13 Per Week. Total Repayable: £2350.14. Fixed Annual Interest Rate: 90.00%. Representative APR 281.59%.

Collections are taken by Gissaloan through your direct debit card. Customers have the option to repay early and clear their account at any time and they may be eligible for a rebate when doing so.

Useful Information About Gissaloan Logbook Loans

Gissaloan charge a £25 fee for every missed repayment if no contact has been received from the customer. Failing to keep up with repayments may lead to the repossession of your car to cover the cost of your loan. Your car can be sold at market value and you may be required to pay the shortfall of any outstanding debt remaining. Once sold, you will have the option to repurchase your car.
