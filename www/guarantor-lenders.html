How the application works for a Guarantor Lender

The main criteria with the guarantor lenders that we feature is that you must be over 18 years of age, a UK resident and earn a minimum income of at least £600 per month. Above all, you require a person to be your guarantor such a family member, friend, spouse or colleague who agrees to repay your loan for you in the event that you cannot meet the repayments.

When you apply through one of our guarantor lenders, you will be required to enter your details on their application form including your name, address, employment, income and bank details. You will also be required to provide details about your guarantor.

Both the borrower and the guarantor will be required to sign a pre- contract agreement confirming their responsibilities and how the guarantor loan works. In some cases, the lender might send a code by SMS to both parties which can be used as a way of signing the loan agreement online.

The checks carried out by Guarantor Lenders

There are several checks carried out by guarantor lenders which include a basic phone call to both the borrower and the guarantor. It is essential that the guarantor understand their responsibility, as they may need to cover the cost of the borrower’s loan. In addition, the guarantor lender will run credit checks on both parties and this is an essential procedure. Guarantor loans are popular for those with bad credit or no credit and have been denied elsewhere. By having a guarantor with a good credit rating, it shows that they have a history of paying loans on time so the lender feels confident that they will be able to retrieve their repayments. Therefore, the better the credit rating of your guarantor, the more likely your loan will be approved.

Further affordability checks will be carried out in order to match what the applicants wants to borrow with how much they can afford to repay. Guarantor lenders will look closely at the levels of income when making their decision but they have to be careful not to lend the borrower too much or they will fall into arrears.

When will I receive my funds?

The companies on our website are able to transfer funds within 1 to 3 working days, depending on the lender. When a loan application is successful, the monies are transferred to the guarantor’s bank account who can decide to pass on the loan to the borrower or change their mind and give the money back to the lender.

How do the repayments work?

Repayments are made in equal monthly instalments and will be collected from the borrower’s debit account. If payment fails, the lender will contact the borrower but if there is no response for a certain period, the repayments may be taken out of the guarantor’s bank account.
