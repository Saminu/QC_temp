Money Boat Loans

Moneyboat.co.uk is an instalment loan lender offering 2-month and 6-month loans of up to £1,500. They have a very competitive APR of 939.5% and their daily interest rate is 0.7% which is lower than average payday lenders.

Moneyboat.co.uk is a direct lender and this means that you can apply directly on their website and if approved, you will receive the funds directly in to your bank account and there are no other middlemen involved. They are based in the centre of London and are operated by Evergreen Finance London Limited. The company do not have a storefront so all applications are handled online or over the phone with their discrete and friendly staff.

How much can I borrow with Money Boat?

First time borrowers with Moneyboat.co.uk can borrow from £200 to £800 subject to relevant credit checks and affordability checks. This will include a review of your credit file to get an idea of your credit history and affordability measures may require proof or confirmation of your salary and income.

Repeat customers can borrow up to £1,500 but this will also depend on your affordability and whether you are able to keep up with the monthly instalments.

How do repayments work with Money Boat?

Repayments with Moneyboat.co.uk are collected automatically via the customer’s account on their payday. Using continuous payment authority, a customer’s account can be automatically debited for collection on the day they receive their salary to ensure a smooth repayment.

Customer repayments are carried out in monthly instalments. The minimum period you can borrow for is 2 months and the maximum is 5 months. Repayments are made in equal monthly instalments.

Moneyboat.co.uk allow you to repay early once you have had the loan open for more than 5 days. Since you are charged a daily interest, it will always be cheaper to repay early.

Representative Example: £400 loan for 113 days (4 instalments), Total: £597.48. Interest £197.48. Interest rate 255.5& pa (fixed). Repay £149.37 for 4 payments.
