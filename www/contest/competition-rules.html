Competition Rules

  • Entry to the competitions is restricted to one entry per person please.
  • Automated entries, bulk entries or third party entries will be disqualified!
  • Competitions are open to UK residents only, over the age of 18, unless otherwise stated
  • Prizes can only be sent to a valid UK address unless otherwise stated.
  • Winners will be chosen at random from all valid entries.
  • Winners will be contacted via email names displayed on our website.
  • The Judges decision is final and no correspondance will be entered in to.
  • The competition will run from the start date displayed on the competition page until closing date.
  • Quiddi Compare is compliant with the data protection act. Our policy is such that we will not pass on your details to any third party without your prior consent.
