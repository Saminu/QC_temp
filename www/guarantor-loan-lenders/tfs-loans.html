TFS Loans

Essex-based TFS Loans have been offering guarantor loans in the UK since 2009. As a guarantor lender, borrowers require a guarantor to be involved in the process who agree to cover the cost of the loan if they are unable to repay. This guarantor is usually a family member, friend or colleague.

TFL Loans lets customers borrow between £1,000 and £12,00 and repay in monthly instalments over the course of 1 year up to 7 years. With a competitive APR of 48.9%, there are no upfront costs for applying and the application is completely entirely online. Simply click through this page to be taken directly to their application form.

Both the borrower and the guarantor will be required to fill in details online including their contact details, income and bank details. If both parties pass the relevant credit and affordability checks, they will be required to E-sign the loan agreement online or by post to complete the transaction. If successful, the funds can be transferred on the same day.

How Much Can I Borrow From TFS Loans?

Customers can borrow up to £12,000 for up to 7 years however this maximum borrowing facility is dependent on a number of factors. By having a higher income or a guarantor with a very good credit history, you are more likely to be approved and borrow more - as lenders like TFS Loans consider this a lower risk of customer default. There may be a limit of how much you can borrow on your first loan but repeat customers that demonstrate a good repayment history may be able to borrow more on their subsequent loans.

What Is The Criteria For TFS Loans?

For a borrower to be eligible for TFS Loans, they must be over 18, a UK resident and in employment with a regular income so that they can afford to repay their loan.

The borrower must have a guarantor willing to repay their loan if they default. TFS Loans only accept guarantor that are homeowners because this demonstrates a better credit rating. Guarantors must also be aged between 18 and 78, have a regular income and a good credit history.

How Do Repayments Work with TFL Loans

Repayments are collected straight from the borrower’s debit account in equal monthly instalments starting from 1 year up to 7 years. Using continuous payment authority, TFS Loans will be able to automatically collect from the borrower’s account on their pay date each month, enabling a smooth repayment process. Borrowers have the option to repay early at any point and it will typically be cheaper to do so.

Representative repayment example: Borrow £4,500 over 60 months. Repay £175.91 per month. Total repayable £10,554.60

The guarantor’s account will not be debited each month, this is only in the case that the customer defaults their repayment and does not respond to contact from the lender. If a borrower fail to make a repayment, the guarantor will be notified within 10 days if they need to make a repayment. The guarantor will only be required to cover the monthly repayment until the borrower can start paying again.

Useful Information About TFS Loans

Borrowers have the choice to cancel their loans 14 days after receiving the funds into their account however you will be required to give back what you borrowed and the daily interest that has accumulated within 30 days.
